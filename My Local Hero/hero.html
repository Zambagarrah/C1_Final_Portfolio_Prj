Dr. Amina Mohamed

Mombasa's Dedicated Healthcare Pioneer and Community Leader.

Learn Her Story

Biography

Dr. Amina Mohamed was born and raised in Mombasa,Kenya. From a young age, she showed exeptional dedication to helping others.often volunteering at local clinics during her school years.

After compliting her medical degree at the university of Nairobi, she returned to Mombasa to serve her community.

She established the first free healthcareclinic in Mombasa's largest informal settlment, providing essential medical servicesto thousands who couldn't afford treatment. Her work has significantly reduced child mortality and improved health in the region.

Dr. Amina Mohamed alizaliwa na kukulia Mombasa,Kenya. Kuanzia umri mdogo, alionyesha kujitolea kusaidia wengine. mara nyingi alijitolea katika kliniki za mitaa wakati wa miaka yake ya shule.

Baada ya kukamilisha shahada yake ya matibabu katika chuo kikuu cha Nairobi, alirejea Mombasa kuhudumia jamii yake.

Alianzisha kliniki ya kwanza ya huduma ya afya bila malipo katika makazi yasiyo rasmi makubwa zaidi ya Mombasa, ikitoa huduma muhimu za matibabu kwa maelfu ambao hawakuweza kumudu matibabu. Kazi yake imepunguza kwa kiasi kikubwa vifo vya watoto na kuboresha afya katika kanda.

Achievements & Impact

Community Clinic

Established 3 free community clinics serving over 50,000 patients annually in undiscovered areas in Mombasa.

Maternal Health

Reduced maternal mortality by 40% in the communities she served through education and accessible prenatal care.

Education

Trained over 200 community health workers who now serve across costal Kenya, multiplying her impact.

Timeline of Achievements

1998

Graduated with honors from the University of Nairobi Medical School.

2002

Opened the first free community in Kibera, Nairobi.

2007

Established the Mombasa Healthcare Initiative focusing on maternal health.

2013

Recived the Presidential Medal of Service for health contributions.

2019

Expanded clinics to serve three additional communities in the coast region.

2022

Launched mobile clinic services to reach remote areas.
